```Markdown
!repeat or !again or !a     - Repeats the currently playing song.
!shuffle or !sh             - Shuffles the playlist queue.
!player [player name]       - Retrieves WoW vanilla player information from http://realmplayers.com.
!raidplayer [player name]   - Retrieves WoW vanilla link to last raid of given player from http://realmplayers.com.
!raidguild [guild name]     - Retrieves WoW vanilla link to last raid of given guild from http://realmplayers.com.
!item [item name]           - Retrieves WoW vanilla item information from http://db.vanillagaming.org.
```
