```Markdown
List of sound commands:
!help sound                 - Prints out this menu.
!yt [youtube link]          - Plays audio from YouTube or other sources in voice channel of caller.
!stop                       - Stops playing sound from the yt player.
!pause                      - Pauses playing sound from the yt player.
!resume                     - Resumes playing sound from the yt player.
!skip                       - Skips to the next playing in the queue if one exists.
!q [(index)]                - Lists the playlist queue. Use reactions to scroll in the queue.
!next                       - Displays the next song in the queue.
!volume or !sv [(volume)]   - Displays and changes the current volume of the current song.
!delete [index]             - Deletes the song with the given index from the playlist queue.
!vote [index]               - Vote for a song to skip the queue. You can only vote for one song once.

Intro commands:
!intro [(index)]            - Plays one of your intros at random. Can play intro at given index.
!introstop                  - Stops playing the currently playing intro.
!myintros                   - Lists all of your intros.
!deleteintro [index]        - Deletes intro at given index.
!upload [url]               - Uploads a intro. File must be .wav and must be downloaded from Dropbox.
The url must end on .wav. Ending with ?dl=0 is not acceptable.
Example: https://www.dropbox.com/s/znt5tt3xe9vl8su/kekkk.wav
```
